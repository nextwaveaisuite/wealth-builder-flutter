About Wealth Builder™

Wealth Builder helps Australians automate small, rules-based contributions across growth/safety ETFs with sensible guardrails. We don’t hold funds or place orders — you execute at your chosen provider (Raiz, Spaceship, CommSec Pocket, Stockspot, QuietGrowth).

  • Plan: Tiny weekly contributions to a target mix (e.g., 70% growth / 30% safety).
  • Allocate: Rule-based splits across ETFs (VAS/VGS/IVV/VAF/GOLD) to reduce drift.
  • Guardrails: Loss Guard floors, caps, and “pause → safety” on rough weeks.
  • Micro-tilts: Tiny, capped tilts that never dominate the long-term plan.
  • Withdrawals: Safety-first sell plan + CSV export; you place orders at your provider.
