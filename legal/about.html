About Wealth Builder™

Wealth Builder™ is a micro-contribution, rules-based investing app for Australians. It focuses on simplicity, safety guardrails, and set-and-forget discipline using ETFs and mainstream micro-investing providers.

What it does

  • Plans small recurring contributions and pulls your mix back to target (e.g., 70/30).
  • Uses sensible floors, caps and Loss Guard to temper risk.
  • Lets you execute at providers like Raiz, Spaceship, CommSec Pocket, Stockspot and QuietGrowth.
  • Exports CSVs for safety-first withdrawals. Future phases will add read-only holdings and—if partnered—API execution.

What it doesn’t do

  • We don’t hold funds or place orders.
  • We don’t give personal financial advice.
